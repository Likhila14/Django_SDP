    Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ'S):
  1. If I cancel an event will I get a refund?

    Answer: It depends upon your cancellation duration from the booking date.

      The refund is as follows :
    1. Full amount refunded if Cancelled within 24 hours
    2. Half the amount will be refunded if canceled before one week
    3. No refund if canceled after less than three days to the event.

  2. How do I book an event?

    Answer: Go to the event page and select an event. You will be automatically directed to the booking page. From there make the payment and you will be receiving an email. You can check the status in the my-profile option.

  3. How do I cancel an event?

    Answer: Goto my-profile page, where you can see events that you have registered. Click on the cancel button before the event that you want to cancel.
